About Me

Hi! I’m Anna, I’m a Product designer who advocates for exceptional design and user experience. I am passionate about designing for both mobile and web. I particularly enjoy collaborating with others to try and create the best product possible that supports the needs of its users. My background in visual design helps me create UI designs that are simple, aesthetically pleasing, and typographically perfect. My purpose as a designer is to directly help others through developing a positive relationship between users and the accessibility of the products they use.
